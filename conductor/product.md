# Product Guide: luxu-real-estate

## Project Overview
luxu-real-estate is a modern real estate application built with Next.js and React, specifically designed for High-end Buyers/Renters. The platform focuses on luxury properties, providing a premium experience for searching, discovering, and managing high-quality listings.

## Target Audience
The primary users are **High-end Buyers/Renters** who are looking for premium real estate properties. These individuals expect a seamless, visually stunning, and highly functional interface to explore their future homes.

## Core Goals
The main objective of the platform is **Property Discovery**. We aim to provide an intuitive and efficient way for users to search for and discover premium properties that meet their high standards.

## Key Features
- **Search & Visualization**: Advanced search filters, high-quality image galleries, and detailed property maps to provide an immersive experience.
- **User Personalization**: User accounts, favorites lists, and personalized profiles to allow users to save their interests and manage their searches.
- **Admin/Management Tools**: A comprehensive admin dashboard for tracking leads and managing property listings effectively.

## Success Metrics
- **Engagement & Quality**: We will measure success through high search-to-view conversion rates and the accuracy and completeness of property listings.

# Product Guidelines: luxu-real-estate

## Branding & Design Style
- **Modern & Professional**: The application should have a clean, efficient, and data-driven aesthetic. The focus is on clarity and ease of information access, ensuring that users can quickly find the luxury properties they are looking for.

## User Experience (UX) Principles
- **Visual-First Navigation**: Prioritize high-quality visuals and intuitive navigation to make the process of searching and discovering properties engaging and immersive.
- **Frictionless Search**: Provide powerful yet easy-to-use filtering options, allowing users to find specific luxury properties with minimal effort.
- **App-Like Responsiveness**: Ensure a smooth, app-like experience with fast transitions and highly responsive interactions across all devices.

## Technical Guidelines
- **Type Safety & Modularity**: Adhere to strict TypeScript usage and maintain a modular, component-driven architecture for scalability and maintainability.
- **Modern Styling & Patterns**: Utilize utility-first styling with **Tailwind CSS 4** and follow the latest **Next.js 16.2.0 (App Router)** patterns and conventions.
- **Performance-Centric Design**: Optimize the application for fast initial page loads and smooth client-side interactions, especially when handling high-resolution property imagery.

## Code Quality & Documentation
- **Clean & Concise Code**: Strive for self-documenting code through clear naming and logical structure. Use comments sparingly but effectively to explain the "why" behind complex logic.
